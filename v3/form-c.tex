  $a \approx b$.  Pick a very large integer $N$.  What can you say
  about $a^N$ and $b^N$?
  \begin{multipleChoice}

    \choice[correct]{$a^N$ might not be very close to $b^N$ even though $a \approx b$.}
  \end{multipleChoice}
